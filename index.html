About Me

Howdy World! I am a front end web developer with experience working with a full stack. I am almost a true Austinite, (I was born in Dallas...please don't tell anyone) I was lucky enough to grow up in Austin and it is the place that I am proud to call home. When I was a kid I watched the city grow and change largely due to the tech industry, now as an adult I want to be a part of that change and make my own contribution to the ever-evolving Austin tech landscape.

After gradutating college I got a job working for the Alamo Drafthouse and contributed to the Austin culture in that way. I am very proud of the time I have spent at the Alamo and the impact that I had on the company as a bar trainer at new venues and as the bar manager at the Ritz. Working at the Alamo proved to be a high-stress and fast paced environment and a great place to learn how to communicate lots of information to lots of different people as efficiently as possible.

The tech industry finally drew me in. With an infinite amount of things to learn, make and share I am excited to work wcollaboratively with diverse groups of people and build cool projects. I have worked with HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, Materialize, JavaScript, Node.js, Express, Handlebars, mySQL, Sequelize, Firebase, MongoDB and React. Please take a look at my portfolio to see some of the stuff I've built.
